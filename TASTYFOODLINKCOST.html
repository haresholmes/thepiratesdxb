BUSINESS OVERVIEW

Tasty Link Food Trading LLC is a professional wholesale food company specializing in fresh meat and poultry products. We are developing a Business-to-Consumer mobile application to enable direct sales of wholesale fresh meat to consumers. The platform will allow customers to browse available meat products, add items to cart, and place orders for delivery to their saved addresses, with instant notifications for order fulfillment and delivery scheduling.
